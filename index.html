Step-by-Step Guide to Activate Your Citi Card at citi.com/activate

  1. Open your web browser and go to citi.com/activate
  2. Enter your User ID and Password.
  3. If you don’t have an online account yet, click “Register” to create one.
  4. Once logged in, look for the “Activate a Card” option on your dashboard.
  5. Provide the required information:
    • Card number
    • Security code (CVV)
    • Expiration date
    • Any other requested personal details
  6. Citi may ask for additional verification (e.g., one-time passcode sent to your phone or email).
  7. Review the details and click “Activate”.
  8. You’ll see a confirmation message once your card is successfully activated.
  9. Your card is now ready for purchases, online transactions, and ATM withdrawals.

Once these steps are completed, your card is ready to use for online shopping, in-store payments, and ATM transactions.
